Welcome to Art World!

Welcome to Art World, a blog I designed to present my latest artwork. Portrait requests are taken every Tuesday, from 2pm to 6pm, and the drawings hopefully finished in a few days. Feel free to send me information about your drawing request via email (my email is kenzy@artworld.com). The first ten drawing requests are taken! I also do Character Designs, which are either drawings of how I imagine characters from books i have read, or just random people.As I'm a very lazy person, sometimes my sketches turn out better than the actual drawing, which is why I decide to keep them the way they are, in case I ruin them by trying to make them look "neater".Typography is also one of the simple things I enjoy drawing. I have just recently started it, so its not as good. I also have "Question Time", which is when I answer any questions you ask. There's really no specific time for it, but it's usually on Saturdays.
